Bengali Shukto

Ingredients

  • Bitter gourd (karela)
  • Raw banana
  • Eggplant
  • Drumsticks
  • Potato
  • White radish
  • Milk
  • Poppy seed paste
  • Mustard paste
  • Spices (panch phoron, turmeric)
  • Ghee

Procedure

  1. Chop the vegetables (bitter gourd, raw banana, eggplant, drumsticks, potato, and radish) into small pieces.
  2. Heat ghee in a pan, add panch phoron, and let it splutter.
  3. Add the bitter gourd and saut√© until slightly golden, then add the remaining vegetables and spices.
  4. Add poppy seed paste, mustard paste, and milk, then cook until the vegetables are tender and flavors are well blended.
  5. Serve hot with steamed rice.
