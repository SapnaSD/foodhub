Gatte Ki Subzi

Ingredients

  • Besan (Gram flour)
  • Yogurt
  • Spices (turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder)
  • Oil
  • Asafoetida (Hing)
  • Mustard seeds, cumin seeds

Procedure

  1. For gatte: Mix besan with spices, oil, and water to make a dough. Roll into logs, boil until cooked, and cut into pieces.
  2. For gravy: Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and asafoetida. Add yogurt and spices, and cook to make a thick gravy.
  3. Add the gatte pieces to the gravy and simmer for a few minutes until they absorb the flavors.
  4. Serve hot with roti or rice.
