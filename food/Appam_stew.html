Appam with Stew

Ingredients

  • Rice flour
  • Coconut milk
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Vegetables (for stew)

Procedure

  1. For appam: Mix rice flour, coconut milk, and yeast to make a smooth batter. Let it ferment for a few hours.
  2. Heat a pan, pour a ladle of batter, and swirl it around to form a thin layer. Cover and cook.
  3. For stew: Cook vegetables in coconut milk with spices. Serve with appam.
