Bengali Mishti Doi

Ingredients

  • Full-fat milk
  • Sugar
  • Yogurt (for fermentation)

Procedure

  1. Boil the milk until it reduces to about half its original quantity.
  2. In a separate pan, caramelize the sugar by heating it until it turns golden brown, then add it to the milk and mix well.
  3. Let the milk cool slightly, then add a small amount of yogurt to act as a starter culture.
  4. Pour the mixture into clay pots, cover, and let it set in a warm place for several hours or overnight until it thickens into yogurt.
  5. Chill before serving.
