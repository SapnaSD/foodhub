Bisi Bele Bath

Ingredients: Rice, Toor dal, Tamarind, Vegetables (carrot, beans, peas), Spices (curry leaves, mustard seeds, etc.), Bisi Bele Bath powder.

Procedure: Cook the rice and dal separately. In a large pot, heat oil, add spices, and saut√© the vegetables. Add tamarind water and Bisi Bele Bath powder. Simmer until vegetables are tender. Add cooked rice and dal, mix well, and let it cook until flavors combine. Serve hot with ghee on top.
