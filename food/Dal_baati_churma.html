Rajasthani Dal Batti Churma

Ingredients

  • Whole wheat flour (for Batti)
  • Ghee
  • Mixed lentils (for Dal)
  • Spices
  • Sugar or jaggery (for Churma)

Procedure

  1. For Batti: Knead the wheat flour with ghee and water to make a dough. Shape into balls and bake until golden.
  2. For Dal: Cook mixed lentils with spices to make a flavorful dal.
  3. For Churma: Crumble some bati and mix with ghee and sugar or jaggery.
  4. Serve the dal with bati, and churma on the side.
