Puttu and Kadala Curry

Ingredients

  • Puttu flour
  • Coconut
  • Black chickpeas (Kadala)
  • Spices

Procedure

  1. For puttu: Steam the puttu flour with grated coconut in a puttu maker.
  2. For kadala curry: Cook black chickpeas with spices, onions, and tomatoes to make a flavorful curry.
  3. Serve the steaming puttu with hot kadala curry.
